Clearcase plugin

Connect with clearcase

in navigator, right click on project top > team > associate with clearcase

After rebase

project>clean

Clearcase toolbar

window > perspective > costumize perspective > clearcase

If you are missing clearcase in toolbar of dvt

Try to add:

org.eclipse.ui.workbench/UIActivities.ro.amiq.dvt.activities.enabled.ClearcaseToolbar=true

 

to ~/.dvt/setteings/preferences.ini
